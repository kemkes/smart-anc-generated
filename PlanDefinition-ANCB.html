workflow ANC.B Routine contact
Jurisdiction: 001
Actions:
Confirm pregnancy: Confirm the woman is pregnant using a urine dipstick test or by a blood test.
Then: Definition: http://fhir.org/guides/who/anc-cds/ActivityDefinition/ANCB4
Quick check: Health worker assesses woman to see whether there are any danger signs that warrant in-facility management or referral, or if possible, to proceed with the routine ANC contact.
Then: Definition: http://fhir.org/guides/who/anc-cds/ActivityDefinition/ANCB5
Collect woman's profile and history: If this is the woman’s first ANC contact, the health worker records information on her current pregnancy, past medical and obstetric history, medications, behaviour, immunization status and other background information.
Then: Definition: http://fhir.org/guides/who/anc-cds/ActivityDefinition/ANCB6
Check symptoms and follow up: Health worker follows up on any previously reported behaviours, medications and symptoms. Steps 8–10 below are all conducted within the ANC assessment.
Then: Definition: http://fhir.org/guides/who/anc-cds/ActivityDefinition/ANCB7
Conduct physical exam: Health worker conducts a physical exam for weight, height (if first contact), maternal exam and fetal assessment.
Then: Definition: http://fhir.org/guides/who/anc-cds/ActivityDefinition/ANCB8
Conduct laboratory test and imaging: Health worker orders or follows up on required laboratory tests and ultrasound, as necessary. Health worker informs woman of all tests ordered and explains why.
Then: Definition: http://fhir.org/guides/who/anc-cds/ActivityDefinition/ANCB9
Counselling, in-facility management and treatment: Based on the previous steps, health worker provides counselling for potential risk, behaviours and diagnoses, as well as preventive services and any treatment that can be provided at the facility, including through admission to a different part of the facility.
Then: Definition: http://fhir.org/guides/who/anc-cds/ActivityDefinition/ANCB10
Referral needed?: If there are any diagnoses requiring referral or services that cannot be provided at the facility, health worker refers pregnant woman based on findings during counselling.
Then: Definition: http://fhir.org/guides/who/anc-cds/ActivityDefinition/ANCB11
Scheduling: If a referral is not required, health worker schedules follow-up contact.
Then: Definition: http://fhir.org/guides/who/anc-cds/ActivityDefinition/ANCB12
