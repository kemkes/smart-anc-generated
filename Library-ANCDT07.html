Library: ANC.DT.07 Ultrasound recommendation

Official URL: http://fhir.org/guides/who/anc-cds/Library/ANCDT07 Version: 0.3.0
Active as of 2023-01-04 Responsible: World Health Organization (WHO) Realm: World Computable Name: ANCDT07
Other Identifiers: id: ANC.DT.07 (use: OFFICIAL)

An ultrasound is recommended for all pregnant women before 24 weeks of gestation (early ultrasound). If there’s no ultrasound available at that health-carte facility, the woman should be referred to another facility in which she can receive an ultrasound.

library ANCDT07

using FHIR version '4.0.1'

include FHIRHelpers version '4.0.1'

include ANCConfig called Config
include ANCConcepts called Cx
include ANCDataElements called PatientData
include ANCContactDataElements called ContactData

context Patient

/*
&quot;Gestational age&quot; &lt; 24 weeks
*/
define &quot;Should Conduct ultrasound at the health-care facility or the referral facility&quot;:
  ContactData.&quot;Gestational age&quot; &lt; 24 'weeks'

/*
&quot;Gestational age&quot; ≥ 24 weeks
*/
define &quot;Should Conduct ultrasound at the health-care facility or the referral facility optional if deemed necessary by the health worker&quot;:
  ContactData.&quot;Gestational age&quot; &gt;= 24 'weeks'

/*
(&quot;Ultrasound scan conducted&quot; = FALSE)
  AND (&quot;Ultrasound available at the health-care facility&quot; = TRUE)
  AND (&quot;Gestational age&quot; &lt; 24 weeks)
*/
define &quot;Should Conduct ultrasound scan&quot;:
  ContactData.&quot;Ultrasound done&quot; is false
    and Config.&quot;Ultrasound available at the health-care facility&quot; = true
    and ContactData.&quot;Gestational age&quot; &lt; 24 'weeks'

/*
(&quot;Ultrasound scan conducted&quot; = FALSE)
  AND (&quot;Ultrasound available at the health-care facility&quot; = FALSE)
  AND (&quot;Gestational age&quot; &lt; 24 weeks)
*/
define &quot;Should Refer to a facility with ultrasound equipment&quot;:
  ContactData.&quot;Ultrasound done&quot; is false
    and not Config.&quot;Ultrasound available at the health-care facility&quot; = false
    and ContactData.&quot;Gestational age&quot; &lt; 24 'weeks'

/*
(&quot;Ultrasound scan conducted&quot; = FALSE)
  AND (&quot;Ultrasound available at the health-care facility&quot; = TRUE)
  AND (&quot;Gestational age&quot; ≥ 24 weeks)
*/
define &quot;Should Ultrasound scan is optional&quot;:
  ContactData.&quot;Ultrasound done&quot; is false
    and Config.&quot;Ultrasound available at the health-care facility&quot; = true
    and ContactData.&quot;Gestational age&quot; &gt;= 24 'weeks'

/*
(&quot;Ultrasound scan conducted&quot; = FALSE)
  AND (&quot;Ultrasound available at the health-care facility&quot; = FALSE)
  AND (&quot;Gestational age&quot; ≥ 24 weeks)
*/
define &quot;Should Ultrasound scan is optional 2&quot;:
  ContactData.&quot;Ultrasound done&quot; is false
    and not Config.&quot;Ultrasound available at the health-care facility&quot; = false
    and ContactData.&quot;Gestational age&quot; &gt;= 24 'weeks'
